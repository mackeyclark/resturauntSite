John's Street Chops is a New York based food cart operating in the South side of Queens and planning to expand into the Bronx. We cater business events, parties, and weddings as well as maintain a consistent workday of 10:00am to 3:00pm. Our meat is 100% USDA choice Beef and Lamb and we offer tofu-based substitues. Our fresh veggies and dairy products are stocked daily. Our carts also sell shirts, hats, armbands, and socks if you'd like to support John's Street Chops!
